## What are the benefits of creating an account?

With an accout you can view your previous and ungoing orders on the site and also be able to subscribe.

### Hot to create an account?

Click the "Create account" button at the top of the home page. On the appeared page, create your account.


## How to buy products?

Open Shops, add their products to the basket then, after you added everything you want to order, click on the basket icon on top of the site and then "Buy products".


## How to become a merchant?

On the account creation page, switch to "Merchant" and create an account. You will need an account to create shops.


## How do I benefit from subscription?

### Customers

Ordering products will become cheaper.

### Merchants

You will be able to create shops.


## How to cancel subscription?

Click on the settings icon on top of the site and here, on the settings page, you can control your subscription plan.
